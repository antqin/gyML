stand with feet slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, bend your elbows and put your hands in front of your chest. flex your hips and squat until your thighs are parallel to the ground, and keep your knees in the same direction as your toes when squatting. when standing up, keep your weight on the outside of the foot, and tense the hip at the side of the support leg; lift the knee on the other leg to the highest point, and twist the body to the side that raises the knee at the same time. lift your knees alternately.
