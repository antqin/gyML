mistake: keep your knees spread out after you lift your body.
solution: keep your knees in the same direction as your tiptoes.
mistake: use your neck to support after the body lift.
solution: use your upper back to support, and do not press your neck.
mistake: lift your hips too high, and turn out your ribs.
solution: tighten your abdominal muscles, retract your ribs, and keep your knees, hips, and shoulders in a straight line.
