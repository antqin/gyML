keep your feet together, tighten your abdominal muscles, and sink your shoulders. hold the dumbbells in both hands on both sides of your body with your fists facing each other. the force is mainly generated by the elbow joint. bend one arm to lift the dumbbell until it is close to the chest while rotating the arm to bring the fist upward; pause for a moment. during the movement, keep your eyes on the front of the big arm on that side. slowly unload the force and return to the starting position, do not fully relax at the lowest point and do not fully straighten the arm; pause briefly to do the other side of the lift.
